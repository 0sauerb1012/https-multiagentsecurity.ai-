This blog is a place for me to write, document, and share my work and research related to multi-agent security (MAS). I work in cybersecurity and have a particular interest in the security implications of agentic AI systems. This includes the emerging field of multi-agent security, which explores how systems composed of collaborating AI agents introduce new risks, architectures, and security challenges. [link].
